A new way to play

Youtopia will ship with a state of the art Neural Gaming Implant (NGI) for players to find themselves immersed in their stories. The NGI sends signals to the brain that stimulate senses such as hearing, smell and sight so players will feel they are a real part of the game. Mobile, PC and Console gameplay is still available.

Real places

Youtopia uses Google Maps data to generate its in-game maps. Players will have the option to choose real world places to explore and start a new life in a real neighbourhood. Visit Rome! Visit Chernobyl, any place is possible!

Any timeline

Choose between 6 different era's! Hide from dinosaurs, be a gladiator, discover the America's or start a rebellion 2000 years from now. Create your story during your favorite time period.
